About Us

CrossChecker is an interactive tool that can be used to verify and fact-check the information, statements and news within Wikipedia articles. It is aimed at combating misinformation on the internet. It can also be a useful tool for students requiring factually correct information for assignments and projects, saving time on having to affirm resources.
